Iced Lemon Tea

Description

Iced lemon tea is a refreshing beverage made by brewing black tea and chilling it, then adding freshly squeezed lemon juice and sweetening it to taste.

Served over ice, this drink combines the bold flavor of tea with the bright, tangy notes of lemon, creating a perfect thirst-quencher for hot days.

Ingredients

  • 4 green tea bags
  • 4 orange pekoe tea bags
  • 6 cups boiling water
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 (12 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate
  • Â½ lemon, juiced
  • 10 cups cold water, or as needed

Steps

  1. Place green tea bags and black tea bags in a 1-gallon glass jar or pitcher.
  2. Pour boiling water over tea bags; steep for 30 minutes.
  3. Remove tea bags; stir in sugar and lemonade concentrate until dissolved.
  4. Stir in lemon juice.
  5. Fill jar to the top with cold water.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator until cold. Serve over ice.
